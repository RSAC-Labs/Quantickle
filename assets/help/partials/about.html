Quantickle v1.0.0

Quantickle is a graph-first investigation workspace built for exploring complex relationships without losing clarity. Use it to ingest data, style it richly, and step through narratives with layouts that fit your story.

Highlights

  • Flexible layouts spanning 2D, 3D, timelines, and globe views.
  • Context-aware editors for nodes, graph areas, and integrations.
  • Import/export tools for CSV, PDF, HTML, and workspace graph files.
  • Security-minded proxy allowlist controls for external requests.
